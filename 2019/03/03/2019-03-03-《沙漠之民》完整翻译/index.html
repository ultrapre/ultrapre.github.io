#词语参照：

克吉鲁 （男人） Kughil

索克托 Soqutian

培吉特 Pejiteto

奇兹塔鲁 Kittarl

Kutin -城市

The capital Pejite,once a thriving city full of life and vigour,was now under the rule of Kittarls. None of its former glory nor peaceful scenery could be seen.

培吉特曾经是一个充满生机和活力的繁荣城市，现在正处于奇兹塔鲁统治之下。 它既没有昔日的辉煌，也没有和平的风景。

To the south of Pejite was Kucchin.It was thelast Soqutian city freefrom the Kittarl clan’ssubjugation.The Kittarl clan hadbeen amassing troopsand supplies in Pejiteto conquer this city.

培吉特南部是Kutin，最后一个未被奇兹塔鲁人占领的索克托城市。奇兹塔鲁人占领了物资，征服了培吉特。

Tem,don’t let your guard down and stick close to me.
铁木，保持警惕，跟紧我。

Outside the city walls were people who were hanged for rebelling against the Kittarls. “Don’t worry,Sasan.I’m sure we’ll find your brother safe and sound.”

在城墙外面有着因反对奇兹塔鲁而被绞死的人。 “别担心，Sasan。我相信我们会安然无恙找到你的哥哥。”

Before any travellers could enter the city,the Kittarls confiscated any weapons that the travellers might be carrying.

在任何旅行者进入城市之前，奇兹塔鲁人没收了旅行者可携带的任何武器。

“Halt!” A Kittarl guard stopped Tem as he tried to enter the city.

“停！”一名奇兹塔鲁警卫阻止了试图进城的铁木。

“It’s only a knife used for cooking and eating,”Tem replied.But talking was useless against the Kittarls. The guards sheathed their swords and were about to kill Tem and Kughil.All of a sudden,someone shouted, “Someone,please stop my camel!It’s gone wild!”
“这只是用来做饭和吃饭的刀子，铁木回答道，但是毫无用处。卫兵们套着剑，准备杀死Tem和克吉鲁。突然有人喊”帮我停一下我的骆驼！它疯了！”

Ha!And just what exactly are you doing with this dagger,you little brat!?

哈！你用这把匕首要干什么，你个小混蛋！？

“I’m terribly sorry.I was careless and acciden- tally lost hold of my camel,”said the boy. The Kittarl guards replied,”Hmph,just be more careful next time!” When the guards turned around from the camel,they realized that the two kids and the man from before had escaped.

“非常抱歉，我的骆驼失控了”男孩说。 奇兹塔鲁卫兵回答说：”哼，下次小心点！”当守卫从骆驼回过神来时，他们意识到前面两个孩子和男人都逃走了。
