2019-07-13-salute-Apollo-11

2019-07-13-salute-Apollo-11

It is just not enough to embalm and recall in Apollo’s 50th anniversary .
阿波罗50周年，仅仅是铭记、回忆是不够的。

The same thing has been done last year and the year before.
千篇一律的事情已经在去年、前年做过了。

However, we have to go further, we must pay tribute ,study and imitate Apollo Project.
然而我们要更进一步，我们要致敬、学习和模仿阿波罗项目。

Having this spiritual core and completing a meaningful action, that is a special tribute approach to the Apollo 11.
拥有这种精神内核，完成一次有意义的行动，这才是一个特别的致敬阿波罗11号方式。

——2019 Just for Apollo 11
