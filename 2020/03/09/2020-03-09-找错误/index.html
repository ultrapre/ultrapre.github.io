UGC: Uppsala General Catalogue of Galaxies

PGC: Principal Galaxies Catalogue
G. Paturel, P. Fouque, L. Botinelli and L. Gougenheim of the Observatoire de Lyon, France, 1989 and 1995. An extragalactic database. I - The Catalogue of Principal Galaxies. Astronomy and Astrophysics Supplement Series, vol. 80, no. 3, p. 299-315 (Nov 1989). Also: Catalogue of Principal Galaxies (PGC). VizieR On-line Data Catalog, VII/119 - online data.

ESO:
A. Lauberts. ESO/Uppsala survey of the ESO(B) atlas. Among the more than 18,000 objects are 85 globular clusters.

Vorontsov-Velyaminov and Arhipova, Moscow State University, 1962-1968, 4 volumes. Contains 29,000 objects. Also referred to as VV.
